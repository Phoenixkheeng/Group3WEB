About Us

MSSN IBBUL CHAPTER is an affiliation of The Muslim Students Society of Nigeria popularly referred to as MSSN was was established on April 18th, 1954 and was officially launched at the Anasruddeen Alakoro Masjid, School Hall, Lagos on 30th May, 1954 with branches in most Tetiary institution and School with the aim of helping Muslim Students both academically and religiously on school campus.
